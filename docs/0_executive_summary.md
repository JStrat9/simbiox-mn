# PROJECT OVERVIEW

## 0. Resumen Ejecutivo

**Nombre del proyecto:** Simbiox

**Versión:** MVP

**Fecha:**

**Responsable:** Joan Lapaix

## Descripción breve (3–5 líneas)

Platform that uses computer vision and real-time feedback to help coaches detect technical errors in human movement during group training sessions.The problem I’m focused on is simple but hard: In gyms, one coach supervises many athletes at once, errors happen fast, and they’re easy to miss. Simbiox runs on fixed cameras + local inference, requiring zero interaction from the athlete, and delivers clear, non-intrusive alerts to the coach. No wearables. No apps for the user. Just better coaching decisions, in real time.
